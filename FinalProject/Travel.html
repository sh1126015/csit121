Certain Tips Before you visit Nepal

Discover the best times to visit Nepal, understand our climate seasons, and plan your trip to coincide with local festivals and events.

Best Times to Visit

The ideal months to visit Nepal are from September to November and from March to May. These months offer the clearest skies and the most favorable weather for trekking and outdoor activities.

Weather Guide

Nepal's weather varies by season:
  • Spring (March to May): Warm with rain showers, and beautiful blossoms.
  • Summer/Monsoon (June to August): Heavy rainfall in most regions.
  • Fall (September to November): Clear skies and moderate temperatures, ideal for trekking.
  • Winter (December to February): Cold, especially in the mountains, with potential snow.

Upcoming Events

Participate in local festivities:
  • Indra Jatra: Celebrated in September in Kathmandu.
  • Holi: The festival of colors, celebrated in March across Nepal.
